# Sage Vault — 3-Minute Demo Script

**Format:** Screen recording + voiceover  
**Target time:** 3:00 minutes  
**Tone:** Confident, calm, product-demo style (think Apple keynote, not tutorial)

---

## PRE-RECORDING CHECKLIST
- [ ] MetaMask open, Base network selected, wallet funded ($1 USDC + ETH for gas)
- [ ] Browser at http://localhost:5173 (or deployed Vercel URL)
- [ ] Browser zoom at 100%, clean tab bar, no notifications
- [ ] Screen resolution: 1920×1080 or 1440×900
- [ ] Microphone tested
- [ ] Close all other apps — no notification popups

---

## SEGMENT 1 — Hook (0:00 – 0:20)

**[Screen: Landing page hero]**

> "Most people want better returns than a savings account — but DeFi is too complex, too risky, and too confusing to trust with real money."

> "Sage Vault changes that. It's an AI-powered DeFi savings account built on YO Protocol — it understands your goals, builds your strategy, and executes it onchain. In under 60 seconds."

*Slowly scroll down the landing page to show the live TVL and APY stats loading in.*

> "These numbers are live — pulled directly from YO Protocol's vaults right now."

---

## SEGMENT 2 — Onboarding (0:20 – 0:55)

**[Click "Start Saving →"]**

> "First time users get a personalized onboarding experience. Sage asks four simple questions."

*Answer the quiz naturally — pick something relatable:*
- Goal: **🌍 Beat Inflation**
- Risk: **⚖️ Balanced**
- Monthly savings: **$500 – $2,000**
- Horizon: **1 – 3 years**

> "That's it. No forms, no KYC, no email. Just four taps."

**[Quiz completes → Dashboard loads with Sage Recommendation card]**

> "Sage instantly generates a personalized allocation — 50% yoUSD for stable yield, 30% yoETH for growth, 20% yoBTC for diversification. With live APYs pulled from YO's vaults in real time."

---

## SEGMENT 3 — Deposit (0:55 – 1:40)

**[Click "Deposit →" on the yoUSD row in the recommendation card]**

> "Every recommendation has a one-tap deposit button. No navigating menus."

**[Deposit modal opens]**

> "I'll deposit $1 USDC into yoUSD — Sage Vault shows me exactly how many shares I'll receive before I sign anything."

*Type "1" in the amount field — watch the share preview update live.*

> "That's a live preview from YO's smart contract — no guessing, no surprises."

**[Click "Deposit $1"]**

> "First, MetaMask asks to approve USDC spending — standard ERC-20 approval."

*Approve in MetaMask.*

> "Now the actual deposit."

*Confirm deposit in MetaMask.*

> "Transaction submitted. On Base, this confirms in about two seconds."

**[Step indicator shows: approving → depositing → waiting → success ✓]**

> "Done. My USDC is now earning yield in YO's risk-adjusted vault."

**[Click "View on Basescan" link]**

> "Every transaction is fully verifiable onchain. This is real — not a simulation."

---

## SEGMENT 4 — Dashboard & Transparency (1:40 – 2:15)

**[Close modal — Dashboard updates with active position]**

> "The dashboard updates instantly. My position is live."

*Scroll down to the Allocation panels.*

> "This is what makes Sage Vault different from every other DeFi savings app — full transparency. You can see exactly where YO deploys your money: Morpho, Lido, Pendle — with live percentages."

> "No black boxes. No 'trust us'. Every allocation is onchain and verifiable."

## SEGMENT 5 — Sage AI + Portfolio (2:15 – 2:45)

**[Navigate to Sage AI page]**

> "Sage isn't just a one-time quiz. It's a persistent AI co-pilot."

*Click a quick prompt: "What if inflation stays at 20%?"*

> "Ask it anything — inflation scenarios, vault comparisons, withdrawal questions. It's grounded in live YO vault data."

**[Navigate to Portfolio page]**

> "The portfolio page has a yield projector — adjust principal, monthly deposits, APY, and time horizon to simulate your savings growth."

*Drag the monthly deposit slider.*

> "This is the kind of tool that makes people trust a product with their savings."

---

## SEGMENT 6 — Close (2:45 – 3:00)

**[Navigate back to Landing page]**

> "Sage Vault is built entirely on YO Protocol — using useDeposit, useRedeem, useUserPositions, useVaultAllocations, and more. Real onchain flows, real yield, real transparency."

> "This is what DeFi savings should feel like."

*Let the hero sit for 2 seconds.*

**[End recording]**

---

## VOICEOVER TIPS
- Speak at 80% of your normal pace — demos always feel faster to the presenter than the viewer
- Don't narrate every click — let the UI speak, you provide context
- If MetaMask is slow, fill the silence: *"Base confirmations are typically under 2 seconds..."*
- If anything goes wrong — stay calm, keep talking, move on

## POST-RECORDING
- Trim dead air at start/end
- Add subtle background music (lo-fi, low volume) if comfortable
- Export at 1080p minimum
- Upload to YouTube (unlisted) or Loom for the submission link
